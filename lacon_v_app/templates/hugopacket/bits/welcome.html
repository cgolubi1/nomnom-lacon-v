Welcome to the 2026 Hugo Voter Packet!

The material in the Packet is provided only for the use of members of LAcon, Worldcon 2026. Please don't distribute Packet materials to others.

You can download Packet materials in the following ways:

  • Download the entire Packet at once.
  • Download the materials for a specific Hugo Award category.
  • Download the materials for a specific finalist.

Evaluation codes are available for some of the Best Game finalists. Codes are limited, so please only request one if you haven't played the game and plan to do so.

The download for each category includes a table of contents document with more information about the materials in that category. Almost every category includes material from all 6 finalists in the category, except for the following: Best Dramatic Presentation, Long Form (5 finalists); Best Game (5 finalists); Professional Artist (5 finalists); and Lodestar (4 finalists).

If you have questions about the Packet or need assistance, please contact us at hugo-help@lacon.org.
